# characters

players choose a certain class for their characters and get random attributes when joining the congqian world, whatever class is chosen cannot be changed unless a player dies and restarts.  each class has its particular attributes and penchants, and could be better suited for specific roles.

## relationships

This signifies the bond between two characters, could be computer or player managed.

Ranges from 0 to 1000, 1000 being the strongest bond.  e.g. Liu Bei, Guan Yu, and Zhang Fei all have 1000 bonds between each other.


## ranks

This depends on the career that is chosen by the character, careers can change at any time, but changing too often causes certain attributes to drop.


## experience

General experience collected for completing tasks.


## wealth

Includes gold taels, land, servants.


## relatives

The connections that you have and bloodlines.
